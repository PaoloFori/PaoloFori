# Hi, I'm Paolo Forin 👋

> PhD Candidate in Neurorobotics & Brain-Machine Interfaces @ University of Padova · Visiting Researcher in Lisbon 🇵🇹

I build real-time **BCI systems** that decode brain signals to control robotic arms, navigate virtual environments, and enable new forms of human-machine symbiosis. My research focuses on **endogenous paradigms** — Motor Imagery and Covert VisuoSpatial Attention — no clicks, no buttons, just thoughts.

Dual M.Sc. in Computer Engineering (AI & Robotics, **110/110 cum Laude**) from UniPD. Strong background in C++/Python, Linux, Docker, and deep learning — I like my pipelines real-time and my containers reproducible.

---

## 🔬 Research

```
Brain  →  EEG  →  Decoder  →  Robot / VR
         (MI · CVSA)   (Deep Learning · ROS2)
```

- 🤖 **Real-time CVSA BCI** — robotic arm control via covert visual attention (QDA + mean power)
- 🤝 **Collaborative BCI** — two operators, one robot, shared control in real time
- 🧩 **Attentional Gating** — GMM-based focus detection for CVSA+MI BCI *(Graz BCI 2026, accepted)*
- 🥽 **BCI in VR** — Motor Imagery + CVSA in immersive environments using VRStream *(Lisbon, 2026)*
- 📊 **ML vs DL for MI BCI** — real-time comparison study *(in progress @ IASLab)*
- 🔁 **ROSNeuro / ROS2Neuro** — open-source BCI middleware contributor
